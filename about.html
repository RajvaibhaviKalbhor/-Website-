About ACMxPCCOER

PCCOER ACM Student Chapter has been founded for the technical development of students in the field of computing. ACMxPCCOER offers numerous workshops and seminars on diverse computing topics, guest lectures from industry experts and academics, and hackathons and competitions that encourage practical skill application and creativity. The Chapter provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.It facilitates community and individual growth in the computing profession.
